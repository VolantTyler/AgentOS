OpenAI announced one of its largest enterprise rollouts with **Samsung Electronics**, while **Satya Nadella** pushed back on a winner-takes-all AI economy and **Margrethe Vestager** reopened EU antitrust scrutiny of **Microsoft–OpenAI** and **Google–Samsung** AI deals. Scale AI and named tech clients did not respond to New Scientist's requests for comment. | [New Scientist — AI training cheating](https://www.newscientist.com/article/2531050-people-training-new-ai-models-admit-they-just-get-chatbots-to-do-it/) |

A Microsoft spokesperson told the Journal the company remains committed to OpenAI and Anthropic partnerships while pursuing a broader frontier ecosystem. | [CNBC TV18 — Nadella interview](https://www.cnbctv18.com/technology/nadella-pushes-back-on-ais-winner-takes-all-future-19929674.htm) |
